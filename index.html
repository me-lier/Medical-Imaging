The project will create a free online platform for analyzing X-rays and brain tumors using AI. Users can securely upload medical images for diagnosis and recommendations. Key aspects include secure cloud storage, AI model integration, and compliance with HIPAA and GDPR. The interface will be user-friendly for easy uploads and clear results.

Project Details

We are committed to aiding the community by offering a cutting-edge tool designed to support medical diagnostics, especially when access to healthcare professionals is limited. Our website features an advanced automated medical imaging analysis system that can evaluate X-ray and brain tumor reports. This innovative project utilizes Kaggle datasets and additional healthcare data to drive its functionality. By harnessing the power of large-scale data, our system aims to significantly enhance medical diagnostics, providing healthcare professionals with valuable insights. The algorithms employed are meticulously trained on diverse and extensive datasets to ensure high accuracy in predictions, achieving an impressive accuracy rate of 92%. Our objective is to democratize access to crucial medical information, making it available to everyone free of charge. We believe that by providing this tool at no cost, we can improve health outcomes and empower individuals with the knowledge needed to make informed decisions about their health. This initiative reflects our commitment to leveraging technology and data to advance healthcare.
